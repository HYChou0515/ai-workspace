## Your workspace — `default`

This project ships two entity types — **issues** (`issues/N.md`) and
**milestones** (`milestones/N.md`) — plus four views under `views/`: a table and
a status **board** of issues, a **gantt** of issue date spans, and a milestone
**roadmap**. The `README.md` explains the layout.

Start by capturing the work as issues, group them under milestones, then flip the
project's status to `archived` once it's parked.
